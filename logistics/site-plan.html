Overview

Purpose

A supply chain and Logistics management company with 9 years’ experience in management of supply chain medical products. Our interest and areas of expertise includes sourcing, warehousing, distribution, and reverse logistics/waste disposal, for government individuals, and private firms. This website is intended to help my clients access my various consultancy services in the the areas of warehousing, health commodities distribution, and human capacity building on logisitics, place order and learn about what we do, so as to enjoy our competitive, quality, and low-cost services.

Audience

Individuals, organizations, agencies, groups, government parastatals, medical homes, situated, and who do business in Nigeria with special interest in supply chain management who deal or work with medical products such as drugs, consumables commodities, equipment, reagent and disposables, within the health sector, who are in search of professional services and consultancy to optimize supply chain systems, minimize cost of procurement, storage, distribution and waste management, and increase system efficiency, and who have access to mobile devices and computer.

Home page

Kinama Logistics services brings to West Africa a modern solution, with innovation in supply chain and logistics management. With over 9 years of experience, Kinama logistics services has been able to develop unique business models to address challenges faced in health commodity supply and logistics management in West Africa, relying on local solutions born out of a systematic approach to problem solving, and at the same time guarantee high quality, efficient and low-cost services for clients within West Africa with special emphasis on distribution of health commodities, warehousing, and human capacity building to improve health supply chain system. Our website is developed, to grant access to our current and potential clients (including consultants, hospitals, clinics, government and private health institutions, individuals, and manufacturers of health commodities) to be able to view our products and services, place request, interact with our agents, view our achievements and innovations in the health logistics field, and track progress of our services to them, real time. Our services

Distribution of health commodities: Kinama Logistics Services with wide range coverage within West Africa, accurate health facility data base and health facility mapping, and having develop efficient route plan tested over the years, is able to employ strategies and delivery models which includes but not limited to, the use of pharma-grade delivery resources to deliver commodities across water, on land and even in hard-to-reach areas to ensure availability of commodities to meet health intervention and needs.

Warehousing: With the commissioning of our 3 major international warehouses and 8 central warehouses in 5 west African countries, we can guarantee you space at standard conditions and low cost, to be able to hold your products with great assurance of safety and accountability. We also offer standard warehousing services and provide technical support to various organization in the equipping and standardization of warehouses for our clients. Kinama Logistics services has also engaged 18 major stakeholders in warehousing to optimize resources and respond timely to request and invitation.

Human capacity building: As an outcome of decades of research into best and promising solutions to local challenges that impact negatively on the capacity of local systems to store, distribute, and manage logistics of health commodities, Kinama Logistics Services has been able to develop training curriculum, strategies, and teaching models to impact knowledge of best practices and solutions to overcome local situations. We have also collaborated with communities, government, and potential client to develop strategies to address training needs that will help support supply chain management in public and private organizations. We also collaborate with training institute and certifying bodies to ensure adequate certification of our training programs. Our training programs are not only theoretically based, but also very practical and delivered through Kinama Logistics Mentorship program.
